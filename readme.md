## 🧠 What is Debugging?

Debugging is the process of identifying, analyzing, and fixing issues (bugs) in your code.  
It’s one of the most important skills that separates beginners from real engineers.

---

## ⚡ Debugging Mindset

- 🧘 Stay calm — bugs are part of the process  
- 🔍 Observe before changing anything  
- 🧩 Break problems into smaller pieces  
- 🧠 Think logically, not emotionally
